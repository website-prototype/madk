2. Exhaustive Categorization of Information We Collect

The operational efficacy of MadKings Esports relies upon the collection of various data points, ranging from explicitly provided personal identifiers to passively accumulated telemetry data. We categorize the information we collect into several distinct taxonomies to better illustrate our data architecture.

A. Voluntarily Submitted Personal Information

This category encompasses any data that you consciously and actively provide to us through direct interactions with our Services. Such instances include, but are not limited to, the creation of a user account, registration for community tournaments, purchasing official merchandise, subscribing to our promotional newsletters, or submitting inquiries to our customer support division. The specific data points within this category may comprise:

  • Identity and Contact Data: Full legal name, preferred pseudonyms or "gamer tags," primary and secondary email addresses, physical billing and shipping addresses, and mobile telephone numbers.
  • Financial and Transactional Data: Credit card numbers, debit card details, third-party payment processor identifiers (e.g., PayPal, Stripe), billing history, merchandise sizing preferences, and comprehensive records of all digital or physical commodities acquired through our platforms.
  • Esports and Gaming Identifiers: In-game player identification numbers (e.g., Riot IDs, Steam IDs, BGMI Character IDs, Epic Games tags), platform-specific usernames (e.g., Discord handles, Twitch usernames, YouTube channel IDs), and historical performance metrics voluntarily linked to your profile.
  • Communication Data: The exact contents, timestamps, and metadata of any correspondence directed to our support staff, community moderators, or business inquiry endpoints.

B. Automatically Aggregated Technical and Usage Data

As is standard practice across robust digital architectures, interacting with the MadKings Esports website triggers the automatic collection of passive data through sophisticated tracking algorithms, server logs, and integrated analytics engines. This data is critical for optimizing user experience, fortifying our cybersecurity posture, and conducting granular demographic analyses. This autonomous collection includes:

  • Device and Telemetry Diagnostics: Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, Media Access Control (MAC) addresses, precise browser types and versions, operating system architectures, screen resolutions, device models, and localized time zone settings.
  • Behavioral Navigation Metrics: Uniform Resource Locators (URLs) indicating the precise referral source that directed you to our platform, the sequential path of pages navigated within our site, the exact chronological duration spent on specific content clusters, interaction rates with embedded multimedia (e.g., VODs, player highlight reels), and specific elements clicked or hovered over during your session.

4. Cookies, Web Beacons, and Advanced Tracking Technologies

The MadKings Esports digital infrastructure heavily relies upon a network of localized data storage mechanisms, colloquially known as "Cookies," alongside equivalent tracking technologies such as web beacons, pixel tags, and local storage objects (LSOs). These technologies are microscopic digital payloads embedded within your browser architecture to facilitate a myriad of functional and analytical processes.

Our implementation of these technologies is categorized as follows:

  • Strictly Necessary/Essential Cookies: These are absolute prerequisites for the foundational operation of our website. They govern session authentication, secure shopping cart persistence, and fundamental security protocols. Without these cookies, the core functionality of the MadKings site would catastrophicly fail.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: We utilize industry-leading third-party analytical suites to aggregate massive datasets regarding user interaction. This allows us to calculate unique visitors, track bounce rates, and comprehend which esports content resonates most profoundly with our audience.
  • Targeting and Advertising Cookies: In collaboration with our esteemed sponsors and advertising networks, these cookies track your cross-site browsing habits to build a comprehensive profile of your interests, thereby allowing the delivery of hyper-relevant advertisements both on our platform and across the broader internet.

Users maintain unilateral control over their cookie environment. Through standard browser configurations, you possess the capability to intercept, block, or systematically purge these tracking mechanisms. However, be advised that the aggressive restriction of essential cookies will inevitably result in a degraded, sub-optimal user experience and the potential failure of critical interactive features.
